# Contested Logistics 

## Overview

Using math programming we are creating resilient logistic plans to expedite and robustify the Marine Corp logistics planning process, taking into account a contested environment.

## Key Findings

Using a robust mixed integer program we can develop a multi time period logistics plan that minimizes the risk any one arc may face by diversifying commodity shipping over time and space. Additionally we can identify arcs that represent bottle necks that are particularly exposed to disruption. At both Pacific Sentry 2025, and LSTX December 2025, we were able to apply our model and directly support our end user.
